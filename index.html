WORK HISTORY AND EXPERIENCE

Most recently at Nitehawk Sweepers, I worked as the parts and service manager. While I was in this role the company exceeded budget in total parts sales, and also saw an increase in profit margins. Before that, I managed a team of installers and fabricators, where we produced a record number of units in both 2017 and 2018! As the production manager I also oversaw the transition of the production floor from a "job-shop" to a lean manufacturing model. This resulted in an increase in production efficency, improved inventory tracking, and a much safer work environment for all employees.
Between 2013 and 2016, I spent time traveling the world offering groundwater surveys with AquaLocate. As the lead field technician I was responsible for operation of the GF-6 seismoelectric exploration technology, including two years of data analysis and interpretation. Earlier, I worked for Centerforce, a local non-profit where I helped adults with developmental disabilities gain employment skills and community access.
I am an analyst and a problem solver, with an eye for detail and a can do attitude. I am skilled with process and procedure improvements, and I'm a natural leader. I am always looking for a new project to tackle, or a way to simply make something better. I believe the diverse and fulfilling work experience I possess is a testament to my capabilities, and with the proper tools and training I can be successful in most any environment.
